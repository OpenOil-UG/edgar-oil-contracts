Corporation 2009 Long-Term Incentive Plan (as amended and restated, the Plan) is amended effective as of March 6, 2013, as follows:   


 
1.
Section 4.1(i) of the Plan is amended by adding the following at the end thereof: 
Notwithstanding the foregoing, any Restricted Stock Award, Restricted Stock Units and Canadian Stock Units that are granted to an Eligible
Director shall, except in the case of such Eligible Directors termination of service due to death disability or an approved reason, or the occurrence of a Change in Control Event, vest not earlier than the first anniversary of the Date of
Grant.   


 
2.
The Plan is not otherwise amended except as provided expressly herein.  [REMAINDER OF PAGE INTENTIONALLY LEFT BLANK]
